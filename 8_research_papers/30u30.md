# Ilya Sutskever's 30 Papers Reading List

Ilya Sutskever, co-founder of OpenAI and one of the most influential figures in AI, famously shared a list of ~30 research papers with John Carmack, stating that mastering these would cover "90% of what matters today" in deep learning.

This document contains that curated list, categorized for easier consumption.

## Foundational Concepts and Architectures

1. **The First Law of Complexodynamics**
   *   *Author:* Scott Aaronson

2. **The Unreasonable Effectiveness of Recurrent Neural Networks**
   *   *Author:* Andrej Karpathy

3. **Understanding LSTM Networks**
   *   *Author:* Christopher Olah

4. **Recurrent Neural Network Regularization**
   *   *Authors:* Wojciech Zaremba, Ilya Sutskever, and Oriol Vinyals

5. **Keeping Neural Networks Simple by Minimizing the Description Length of the Weights**
   *   *Authors:* Geoffrey Hinton and Drew van Camp

6. **ImageNet Classification with Deep Convolutional Neural Networks (AlexNet)**
   *   *Authors:* Alex Krizhevsky, Ilya Sutskever, and Geoffrey E. Hinton

7. **Dropout: A Simple Way to Prevent Neural Networks from Overfitting**
   *   *Authors:* Nitish Srivastava, Geoffrey E. Hinton, Alex Krizhevsky, Ilya Sutskever, and Ruslan Salakhutdinov

8. **Deep Residual Learning for Image Recognition (ResNet)**
   *   *Authors:* Kaiming He, Xiangyu Zhang, Shaoqing Ren, and Jian Sun

9. **Identity Mappings in Deep Residual Networks**
   *   *Authors:* Kaiming He, Xiangyu Zhang, Shaoqing Ren, and Jian Sun

10. **Multi-Scale Context Aggregation by Dilated Convolutions**
    *   *Authors:* Fisher Yu and Vladlen Koltun

11. **Neural Turing Machines**
    *   *Authors:* Alex Graves, Greg Wayne, and Ivo Danihelka

12. **Attention Is All You Need**
    *   *Authors:* Ashish Vaswani, Noam Shazeer, Niki Parmar, Jakob Uszkoreit, Llion Jones, Aidan N. Gomez, ≈Åukasz Kaiser, and Illia Polosukhin

13. **Neural Machine Translation by Jointly Learning to Align and Translate**
    *   *Authors:* Dzmitry Bahdanau, Kyunghyun Cho, and Yoshua Bengio

14. **Scaling Laws for Neural Language Models**
    *   *Authors:* Jared Kaplan et al.

15. **A Tutorial Introduction to the Minimum Description Length Principle**
    *   *Author:* Peter Grunwald

16. **Kolmogorov Complexity and Algorithmic Randomness**
    *   *Authors:* A. Shen, V. A. Uspensky, and N. Vereshchagin

17. **Machine Super Intelligence**
    *   *Author:* Shane Legg
